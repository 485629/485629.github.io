Hello, welcome to the Tilt Maze Game! The main goal of the game is to collect all small black marbles with the big red one. When you move to some direction, you move until you hit an obstacle. I designed three new game extensions named "One Player As Two", "Teleports" and "Switch And Gates". I would like you to try them out and provide some feedback if possible. For understanding the basic game, you can find some easy basic maps in "Tutorial" folder. After you grasp the concept of the game, you can change the folder to some new extension and try it out. Controls: arrow keys for moving the marble to four cardinal directions, r for restart.

Congratulations, you won!
